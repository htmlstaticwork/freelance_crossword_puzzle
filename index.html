Workflow

The Grid's Journey

01

The Concept

Every great puzzle begins with a hook — a theme that lets the grid tell a story. Finding that anchor is the most creative, and most demanding, part of the work.

02

Grid Construction

Placing black squares with surgical precision to maximise "cross-ability" while letting the theme entries breathe. A labour of logic and craft.

03

Clue Crafting

Writing witty, accurate clues matched to the intended difficulty level. Each clue is revised until it is simultaneously fair, clever, and impossible to forget.
